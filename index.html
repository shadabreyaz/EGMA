ABOUT EGMA

We started EGMA to solve a simple problem: eyewear that was overpriced and underwhelming. Today, we serve thousands of customers with high-quality, precision-crafted ophthalmic lenses designed for real life. Using advanced German technology and machinery, and backed by over 50 years of trust and expertise, EGMA specializes in creating lenses tailored for the Middle East market. Our innovative coatings and treatments are meticulously tested to ensure clarity, comfort, and durability in every pair.

Each lens reflects our commitment to optical excellence, precision, and visual well-being. By continuously investing in research and advanced manufacturing, we deliver lenses that redefine vision quality and comfort, combining craftsmanship and technology for lasting clarity and confidence.

Frequently asked
questions

What makes EGMA lenses different from others?
EGMA lenses combine German technology with decades of expertise to deliver precision, comfort, and durability. Designed for the Middle East, they offer superior protection against UV rays, blue light, glare, scratches, dust, and water, ensuring long-lasting, clear vision.
Can I order customized prescription lenses through EGMA?
Yes. EGMA offers customized prescription lenses with various coatings and treatments, tailored to your prescription and designed for the Middle East’s climate. You can order them through authorized retailers or optical stores.
Are EGMA lenses suitable for Middle East weather conditions?
Yes. EGMA lenses are specifically designed and tested to withstand the Middle East’s harsh climate, including heat, dust, and sunlight, while maintaining clarity, comfort, and durability.
Do EGMA lenses come with a warranty or replacement policy?
Yes. EGMA lenses come with support and replacement options through authorized retailers to ensure customer satisfaction.
Where can I purchase EGMA lenses or find authorized partners?
You can purchase EGMA lenses through authorized retailers and distributors in the Middle East, including Dubai Optical, EGMA Optical Supplies, and Rivoli Group.
